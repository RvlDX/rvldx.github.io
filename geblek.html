About Our Geblek

Geblek is a traditional Javanese snack that has been enjoyed for generations in the Sokoagung region. Made from fresh cassava and carefully selected spices, our geblek offers a perfect balance of crispy exterior and chewy interior.

Our family recipe dates back to 1952, preserving the authentic taste while maintaining strict quality standards. We use only locally sourced ingredients, supporting regional farmers and ensuring freshness in every bite.
